---
term: Audio-language model (ALM)
product: windchime
definition: >-
  A model that maps text and audio into one shared space, so a phrase can retrieve matching
  sound. Windchime treats the active ALM as an exchangeable variable behind a single embedding
  interface; the audit compares configurations of public ALMs (CLAP variants, CLaMP 3, and
  others), six of them deployable in the installation.
related: [clap, embedding-space, retrieval]
---
