summary: >-
  The selection policy admits at most one stem per instrument role, which
  guarantees timbral variety but also shapes what the corpus can offer. The
  planned ablation removes the role constraint and runs retrieval over an
  unsegmented corpus, to separate what the audio-language models do from what the
  policy imposes on top of them.
